The Best Rolled Sugar Cookies

This is the best sugar cookie recipe and the only one I use. Whenever you make these cookies for someone, be sure to bring along several copies of the recipe â€” you will be asked for it, I promise! Note: I make icing with confectioners' sugar and milk. I make it fairly thin, as I "paint" the icing on the cookies with a pastry brush. Thin enough to spread easily but not so thin that it just makes your cookies wet and runs off.

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla extract
  • Flour
  • Baking powder
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Cream the butter and sugar
  2. Add eggs and vanilla
  3. Add flour, baking powder, and salt
  4. Cover and chill the dough
